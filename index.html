        Password Security Tips

        Length Matters

        Favor passwords with at least 12 characters. Longer passwords are exponentially harder to crack.

        ‚å®
        Avoid Patterns

        Keyboard walks (qwerty, asdf) and obvious sequences (1234, abcd) are easily guessed.

        No Personal Data

        Don't embed names, birthdays, or email addresses in your passwords.

        Mix Character Types

        Combine uppercase, lowercase, digits, and special characters for maximum unpredictability.

        Unique Per Service

        Never reuse passwords. A breach in one service shouldn't compromise all your accounts.
